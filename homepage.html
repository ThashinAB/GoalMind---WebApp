GoalMind - Football Player Position Prediction

Welcome to GoalMind, a web app that predicts a football player's position based on their physical, mental, and technical attributes. The prediction is made using a neural network trained on a large dataset of football players from various leagues around the world. Please note that technical skills are rated on a scale of 1 to 100. Simply enter the player's data based on their talent, and our neural network will predict the player's position. Start predicting now!
